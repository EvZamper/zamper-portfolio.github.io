About me

I'm a graphic designer specialized in digital development and industrial design areas. I have experience in UX and UI design, front-end and back-end. Extensive knowledge in marketing as a creator of visual social media content.

Prior to UX Design, I’ve worked in tv, editorial and illustation content, public radio and as a social comunication designer. Everything I’ve done has been in pursuit of understanding people more deeply and fostering moments of empathy, sometimes with cience, art and storytelling.

As a designer, I'm interested in how to create powerful, intuitive and enjoyable experiences; my passion is to create projects that in a multidisciplinary way propose and generate solutions through visual strategies applied to graphic design and web design. I hope to bring my perspectives and experiences to a team that thrives on diversity of thought, impactful work and seeks for the benefit of society.

Process

Every project is going to be different, but this is the usual structure that I like to follow as best practices.

Research

Understand the client's goals and objectives of the project. Along with developing mood boards based off of the client's vision.

Concept

Use the research and develop 2-3 rough concepts. Then review with the client to finalize a direction.

Design

Use the research and develop 2-3 rough concepts. Then review with the client before moving onto the next phase.

Review

Back and forth feedback with the client regarding design or copy changes before the finalization of the assets. Up to 2-3 versiond of proofs.

Finalize

Once the project is approved, all the assets will be finalized, files packaged up and sent off to the client. Continuing support is always an option.
